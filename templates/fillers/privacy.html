Fintrix Consulting Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 26, 2025

1. Information Collection

When you use our guest checkout service, we collect:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number for service delivery
  • Payment Information: Processed securely via our payment gateway (we don't store card details)
  • Service Details: Specifics to the service requested

2. Use of Information

We use your information exclusively for:

  • Providing and managing service
  • Processing payments and sending service confirmations
  • Communicating about your service (appointments, updates)
  • Improving our services and customer experience
  • Complying with legal obligations related to services

We never sell your information to third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement robust measures to protect your information:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmissions
  • Regular security audits of our systems
  • Limited employee access to customer data
  • Secure payment processing compliant with PCI DSS standards

4. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Service records: 7 years (for tax and legal compliance)
  • Contact information: Until service completion + 1 year
  • Payment records: As required by financial regulations

You may request earlier deletion of non-essential data.

5. Your Rights

you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

Exercising Your Rights

To make any requests regarding your data, please contact us at consultingfintrix@gmail.com with "Data Request" in the subject line. We may need to verify your identity before processing requests.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be:

Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
